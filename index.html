THE CAPITALIST QUIZ
We were all thinking it, but which of these fictional characters said it so eloquently?
Select the wordsmith behind each quote. Was it Donaghy, Lucille, Cartman, or Trump? Reveal a golden toilet and you're smart enough to be president. Otherwise, brush up, simpleton... what are you, a farmer? Hover your mouse over a character's name to learn more, or hit the money to begin.

ⓘ DONAGHYAh, Jack Donaghy. Star of 30 Rock, head of NBC, and former VP of Microwave Oven Programming. The self-assured businessman is part of the political and economic elite that you know nothing about.
ⓘ LUCILLEThe queen herself, Lucille Bluth. Star of Arrested Development, husband to George Sr., and mother of four. She's proud of her status. Off-put by immigrants.
ⓘ CARTMANThis is Cartman. You may recognize him from South Park. He's a foul-mouth fourth grader. Perpetually insecure and yet refreshingly opinionated. Don't get him started on hippies.
ⓘ TRUMPStar of The Apprentice. Running for president. "He actually has some really good points," says your drunk uncle. Thank god he's imaginary.
